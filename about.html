About The BEST BANK NERD

WHAT?

Best Bank Nerd is a blog aiming to test and review banks and other financial tools. For the most part this blog will be analyzing each subject from a technological stand point. In the quest to find the best bank, or banking tools, out there: I believe that technology and simplicity holds the answer.

WHY?

Hello, my name is Connor! I'm a computer science student and business owner with an eye for simplicity. Banking is one of those things which frustrates the hell out of me, so I thought I'd post my findings for the world to see. I'm a strong believer that technology empowers us, and what you'll find on this site is all about the best technology in banking / finances.
